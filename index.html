media

My research and findings have been featured in internationally renowned media, such as Nature, Washington Post, New York Times, MIT Technology Review, Wired, Bloomberg, Business Insider and TechCrunch, as well as in Le Figaro and La Repubblica.

I wrote two op-eds: one on how debating whether AI is conscious is a distraction from real problems, and another one on Popper's "paradox of tolerance" related to content moderation. I also discussed the pitfalls of fear-mongering in AI in my Analytics India Magazine profile.

I've taken part in TV shows and helped put together investigative reports, notably for BFMTV, TF1, RAI and France24.

Additionally, I enjoy joining podcast & radio discussions, where I disseminate my findings and experiences related to AI, aiding in the demystification of the subject.
